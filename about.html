About

we’ve acquired Renomark, BBB, and BILD certifications, DEMONSTRATING  OUR UNMATCHED DEDICATION AND REPUTATION FOR RELIABILITY.

SK-Aeros is a philippine based renovation company that strives to make homes and workplaces better. We offer a broad range of interior and exterior residential renovation services, including office renovations, kitchen renovations, basement renovations, bathroom renovations, theatre room renovations, games room renovations, laneway housing design and build, home soundproofing, additions, tiny home building and custom home building. All our team members are skilled, insured and certified, bringing with them a huge amount of experience in the field.

Our time-tested, comprehensive approach will assure you that you are valued and your unique ideas will be transformed into a reality. We’re passionate about what we do, and our primary aim is to help you achieve your goals!

At SK-Aeros, we believe that there is a better way to renovate your space.

To ensure that we provide our clients with an amazing journey from start to finish, we have built a custom application dedicated to our clients which allows them to log in to a personalized account to view live updates, photos, videos, and comments related to their projects from start to finish. As well, documentation correspondences, emails, change orders, etc., are documented on this platform to ensure that our clients’ experience is organized and well-communicated for the success of their project. Further, all our dedicated team members, including managers, project managers, and onsite supervisors, utilize the same system to communicate and update on the various projects daily.
