SK AERO Builders

is a construction company based in the Philippines. We offer a high degree of flexibility on a wide range of services such as architectural and engineering services for residential, leisure, commercial, infrastructure and industrial projects to suit clients' requirements.

These services are both effective and cost efficient. We always provide the best services to our clients.

We deliver an integrated service to clients, From feasibility study planning and design, engineering, construction and commissioning, clients are assured of fast and efficient services.

Commitment to Execution/ Planning
It is a fundamental and challenging activity in the management and execution of construction projects. It involves the definition of work tasks, the estimation of the required resources and durations for individual tasks. and the identification of any interactions among the different work tasks. A good construction plan is the basis for developing the budget and the schedule for work. Developing the construction plan is a critical task in the management of construction, even if the plan is not written or otherwise formally recorded. In addition, it may also be necessary to make organizational decisions about the relationships between project participants and even which organizations to include in a project. Punctuality
